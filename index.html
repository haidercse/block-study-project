Meeting the Giants

One moonlit night, I met three giants who taught me silence, strength, and courage.

The First Giant

He spoke slowly, like stone grinding on stone, and asked me what I was building. I said “websites.” He laughed — the kind of laugh that shakes trees.

The Second Giant

This one didn’t speak at all. He just looked at me, and in that silence I realized how loud my thoughts were.

The Third Giant

He gave me no advice. Only a nod, as if to say: keep going. The path is yours now.

About This Tale

"Meeting the Giants" is a short imaginative retelling that blends quiet moments and gentle humor. These giants teach patience, courage and the art of silence. Below is a small peek into the world and a little funny aside.
